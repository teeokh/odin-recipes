Pounded Yam

Description

Pounded yam is a traditional African dish that hails from Nigeria and some other West African countries. It is a popular and well-loved staple food in the region. The dish is made from yam, a starchy tuberous root vegetable that is cooked, pounded, and molded into a smooth, stretchy, and dough-like consistency. Pounded yam is known for its smooth texture and subtle, slightly sweet taste, making it a versatile accompaniment to a wide variety of soups and stews.
